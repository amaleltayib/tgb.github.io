Our Programs

STEM Outreach Program


We aim to increase accessibility to STEM for disadvantaged students by providing free workshops, mentors, and learning resources. We hope to transfer our love for STEM to them.


School Supply Fundraising


We donate school and makerspace supplies to low-income schools by organizing fundraisers, partnerships, and more, aiming to encourage youth exploration and innovation in STEM.


At-Home STEM Kits!


We design engaging, hands-on "kits" or activities for elementary students that incorporate STEM concepts. We aim to inspire curiosity and learning inside and outside of the classroom.
