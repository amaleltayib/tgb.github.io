Bridging Gaps in STEM Education.

​The Golden Beaker is a student-led nonprofit dedicated to increasing STEM accessibility to disadvantaged students. We strive to provide resources, programs, and support to ensure all students have the opportunity to engage with and excel in science, technology, engineering, and math.
